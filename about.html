About R.I.S.E Foundation

The R.I.S.E Foundation is a non-profit organization officially registered under CAC RC No. 8428402. We are dedicated to improving social welfare in disadvantaged communities, particularly in rural areas of Nigeria.

Our Mission

To alleviate social and economic hardship by providing essential relief, sustainable support, and opportunities for long-term empowerment. Through targeted initiatives in food security, education, healthcare, and community development, we strive to uplift underserved populations, foster resilience, and create lasting change.

Our Vision

A self-sufficient and empowered society where every individual has access to basic necessities, quality education, and the resources needed to build a better future. We envision communities where poverty is significantly reduced, opportunities for growth are abundant, and people are equipped with the tools to break the cycle of hardship and achieve lasting prosperity.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We approach every situation with empathy and understanding, treating all beneficiaries with dignity and respect.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our operations and interactions.

Community

We believe in the power of collective action and work closely with local communities to create sustainable change.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all our programs and continuously seek ways to improve our impact.
